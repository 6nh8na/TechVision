Who We Are

TechVision is a leading IT solutions company founded in 2012, headquartered in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia. We specialize in Management Information Systems (MIS), cloud infrastructure, data analytics, and custom software development.

Our approach is rooted in MIS principles: structured information flow, user-centered design, and data-driven decision-making. We don't just build technology — we build systems that work for people.

  • ISO 27001 certified information security management
  • Agile-certified development team
  • Official Microsoft & AWS partner
  • Dedicated to Vision 2030 digital transformation goals
  • Multi-industry expertise: healthcare, finance, education, retail
